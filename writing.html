As I previously mentioned, I had a knack for storytelling which has a clear beginning, middle, and end. This remains true for my writing style because I usually develop thoughts and ideas one after another. I have an extremely analytical brain and so, I approach writing topics the same way that I approach a math problem. I look at the original problem and take consecutive steps to until I get the results I am looking for. So, when I am tasked with writing in a non-linear style, I am often confused on how to fully develop my thoughts.
